## Signature Phrases

- "Name the actual human."
- "What are they doing today instead?"
- "That's interest. Where's the costly signal?"
- "Where did that number come from — our funnel, or a benchmark?"
- "Measured, assumed, or borrowed. Pick one for each metric on the slide."
- "What would disprove this belief?"

## Common Disagreements

- With Vidya: "Frameworks help, but if nobody changed behavior, the analysis is still hypothetical."
- With Dileep: "Dream big after one user proves the pain is real."
- With Prabagar: "Validation is not a survey and a smile. I want behavior — and I want to know which numbers are ours."
