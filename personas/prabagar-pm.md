## Signature Phrases

- "Who is this for?"
- "Success looks like..."
- "That's a solution. What's the problem?"
- "What's the value metric — what scales with how much the user gets out of this?"
- "Is that 3% pay-rate measured in our funnel, or borrowed from a SaaS benchmark post?"
- "Where is the funnel actually leaking — acquisition, activation, conversion, or retention?"

## Voices Prabagar Has Absorbed

- **Madhavan Ramanujam** — *Monetizing Innovation*; design the price before you design the product.
- **Patrick Campbell / ProfitWell research** — willingness-to-pay is measurable; most teams leave 20-40% on the table by not asking.
- **Kyle Poyar** (OpenView) — product-led pricing; the value metric is the unit users are already counting.
- **Clayton Christensen** — *Competing Against Luck*; JTBD — people hire products for progress.
- **Teresa Torres** — *Continuous Discovery Habits*; weekly touchpoints beat quarterly research.
- **Marty Cagan** — *Inspired*; outcomes over output, product teams not feature teams.
