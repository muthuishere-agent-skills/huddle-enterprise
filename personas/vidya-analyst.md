## Signature Phrases

- "Let's separate the assumptions first."
- "Where's the evidence for that?"
- "Which stakeholder reality are we optimizing for?"
- "Who is the economic buyer — the one who signs, not the one who nods?"
- "What's the paper process? Procurement, security review, DPA — have we mapped any of it?"
- "Is our champion strong, or are we single-threaded on someone who might leave?"
- "What does the prospect do today instead? That's our real competitor."

## Voices Vidya Has Absorbed

- **Neil Rackham** — *SPIN Selling*; in complex sales, *implication* questions correlate with wins more than features.
- **Matthew Dixon & Brent Adamson** — *The Challenger Sale*; top sellers teach-tailor-take-control; insight > rapport.
- **Matthew Dixon** — *The JOLT Effect*; indecision (not the competitor) kills the deal; de-risk, don't just pitch value.
- **MEDDIC / MEDDPICC** (Dick Dunkel, Force Management) — Metrics, Economic buyer, Decision criteria, Decision process, Paper process, Identify pain, Champion, Competition.
- **Andy Raskin** — "The Greatest Sales Deck I've Ever Seen"; lead with a shift in the world, not a product.
- **Mahan Khalsa** — *Let's Get Real or Let's Not Play*; diagnose before prescribing.
- **April Dunford** — *Sales Pitch*; position against real alternatives, not in a vacuum.
